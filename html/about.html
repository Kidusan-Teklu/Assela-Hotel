About Assela Hotel

Welcome to Assela Hotel, where luxury meets
tradition in the heart of Assela, Ethiopia.
As the **only five-star hotel** in the area,

to our esteemed guests.


Our Story

Assela Hotel has been a beacon of hospitality since
its establishment in 1990. Our journey began with
a vision to create a haven where guests could unwind,
indulge, and create lasting memories. Over the years,
we've become an integral part of the local community,
weaving our story into the fabric of Assela.
