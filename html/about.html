About Assela Hotel

Welcome to Assela Hotel, where luxury meets
tradition in the heart of Assela, Ethiopia.
As the only five-star hotel in the area,
we take pride in offering unparalleled experiences
to our esteemed guests.


Our Story

Assela Hotel has been a beacon
 of hospitality sinceits establishment
  in 1990. Our journey began with
a vision to create a haven where guests
could unwind,indulge, and create lasting
memories. Over the years,we've become an
 integral part of the local community,
weaving our story into the fabric of Assela.
